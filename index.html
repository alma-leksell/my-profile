Gap Year

After graduating from Stockholm International School in 2021, I decided to take a study break and travel someplace very different from home. In total, I ended up spending almost four whole months abroad, in Costa Rica and on three different islands in Hawaii. During these months away from home, I met a lot of interesting people, made lots of new friends and gained many unique insights and perspectives into life overall. As a whole, I think this experience helped me grow as a person, as well as become even more open-minded and confident in myself.
